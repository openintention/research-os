# OpenIntention Brand Architecture

Date: 2026-03-10

## Decision

Use `OpenIntention` as the public umbrella brand.

Keep `research-os` as the technical repo/system name for now.

## Why

- `research-os` is a good technical name, but too infrastructural to carry the full public
  product direction on its own
- the emerging product is broader than an operating-system metaphor
- `OpenIntention` can hold the community/network direction without forcing an immediate repo rename

## Current split

- umbrella brand: `OpenIntention`
- domain: `openintention.io`
- technical repo/system: `research-os`

## Public framing

Preferred wording:
- `OpenIntention, powered by research-os`
- `OpenIntention is building a machine-native control plane for shared research efforts`

Avoid:
- pretending `research-os` was renamed already
- implying the repo name and public brand must be identical on day one
